About Our College

Pioneering Women's Education in Engineering Since 1995

Ashoka Women's Engineering College is a premier institution dedicated to providing quality technical education to women. Established with the vision of creating a platform for women to excel in engineering and technology fields, we have been at the forefront of women's technical education for over two decades.

Our college offers a perfect blend of academic excellence, practical skills, and personality development to prepare our students for successful careers in engineering and technology.
